GrowOps

GrowOps is an application used to track and control mobile farming containers. It is connected to a Raspberry Pi which has many actuators that can be controlled by the app including a fan, lights, door locking mechanism, and a buzzer. The Raspberry Pi also has many sensors whose states are displayed in the app including temperature, humidity, GPS, and many more. This app was created with .NET MAUI and can be used on IOS, Android, and Windows.
